Vardo

2017–2019

I was involved with Vardo from about 2017–2019. I contributed drum set, percussion, music composition, music arrangement, audio engineering, and audio editing to the studio recordings of the Once Upon A Time In The Midwest EP and its later re-releases, the Curio EP, and the Duende EP. I also contributed live drum set and percussion for Vardo performances at Tesla Con.
