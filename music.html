Other one-off projects

Vardo and related work

I was involved with Vardo from about 2017-2019. I contributed drum set, percussion, music composition, music arrangement, audio engineering, and audio editing to the studio recordings of Once Upon A Time In The Midwest EP (and its later re-releases), Curio EP, and Duende EP. I also contributed live drum set and percussion for Vardo's performances at Tesla Con.
