Bio

I am a sociologist and musician currently based in Bellingham, WA.

Music is my artistic passion. My primary instruments are drum set, keys, and synthesizers. I have in-depth training and experience with music production, music composition, audio engineering, and sound design. I have a discography of solo music releases as well as other projects I have worked on, including Cold Sludge and Vardo.

Sociology is my academic passion. I am a published sociologist, and I am applying to Sociology PhD programs for Fall 2026. I hope to expand research on the sociology of psychedelics. My primary sociological motivations are to directly address material injustice and institutional dysfunction.
